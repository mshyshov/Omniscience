Omniscience - The Book

Imagine a book that contains every piece of possible knowledge ever. A book which has the Bible, Encyclopaedia Britannica, deciphered Indus Valley civilization writings and decoded Voynich manuscript. You will find it here. The very source code of the universe is just a couple clicks/taps away.

Would you dare turn its pages?

Inspired by "The Library of Babel" by Jorge Luis Borges

This application/website/book/codex contains a fixed amount of information. Its pages are not random however, every page has a number and can be seen at any time.

Total amount of information in the book is 5.3308253643 * 10^1468 characters which is overwhelmingly larger than the number of atoms in the known universe.

Every page is 1024 characters long and contains lowercase English (Latin) alphabet letters and space.
